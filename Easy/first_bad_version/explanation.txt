Pattern: 
Separate good and bad using binary search, if the middle is good, then move left to mid to eliminate
the good, but if it is bad, move right to mid to keep searching the lowest bad.

Data structure: None.

Invariant: 
At any time, the answer (first bad version) lies within [left, right].
All versions before a known-good mid are excluded.
All versions from a known-bad mid are still candidates.

Time complexity: O(log n)
