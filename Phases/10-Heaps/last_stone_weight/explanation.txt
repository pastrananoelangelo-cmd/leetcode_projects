Pattern: Store all stones in a max heap, then repeatedly remove the two heaviest stones, 
smash them together, and insert the remaining stone (if any) back into the heap.

Data structure: Max Heap (PriorityQueue)

Invariant: 
At the start of each iteration, maxHeap contains exactly all of the stones 
that still exist after the previous smashes, and the largest remaining stone 
is at the top of the heap.

Time complexity: O(n log n)
