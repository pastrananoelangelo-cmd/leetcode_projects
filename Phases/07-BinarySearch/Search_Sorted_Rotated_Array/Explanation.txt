Pattern: 
At every iteration, at least one half
[left...mid] or [mid...right]
must be sorted.

The target, if it exists, is always preserved
inside the remaining search range.

Data Structure: None.

Invariant: One side (From Left to Mid or Mid to Right) is definitely sorted.

Time Complexity:
O(log n)

Decision Tree:
Is left sorted?
│
├── Yes
│   ├── Target inside?
│   │     └── Search left
│   └── Otherwise search right
│
└── No → right sorted
    ├── Target inside?
    │     └── Search right
    └── Otherwise search left
