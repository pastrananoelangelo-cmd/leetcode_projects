Pattern: 
Compare middle with its next element.

If nums[mid] < nums[mid+1]:
→ we are ascending
→ a peak must exist on the right
→ search right

If nums[mid - 1] > nums[mid]::
→ we are descending 
→ a peak must exist on the left
→ search left

Else: nums[mid - 1] < nums[mid] > nums[mid + 1]
→ it is the peak.
→ return the peak.

Data structure: None.

Invariant: 
There is always at least one peak
inside the current search range.

Then:
Ascending at mid
→ peak cannot disappear on right

Descending at mid
→ peak cannot disappear on left

Time complexity: O(log n)
