Pattern: 
Maintain a structure of size k that always contains the k largest elements seen so far.
If a new element is larger than the smallest in the structure, replace it.

Data structure: Heap.

Invariant: 
The heap stores the k largest elements,
and its top is the smallest among them,
which is exactly the k-th largest overall.

Time complexity: O(n log k), where k is the number of k (heap).
