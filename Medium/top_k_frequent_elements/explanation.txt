Pattern: 
1. Count frequencies with a HashMap.
2. Maintain a min heap of size k containing the k most frequent elements.

Data structure: 
HashMap<Integer, Integer>
Min Heap of (number, frequency) pairs (PriorityQueue<int[]>)

Invariant: 
After processing some entries of the frequency map, the heap contains the 
k most frequent (number, frequency) pairs seen so far, with the smallest 
frequency among them at the root.

Time complexity: O(n log k)
